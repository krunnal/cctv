Frequently asked questions :

**[ Please access this site in 'Firefox' or 'Google Chrome' only. ]

1. Can I use CCTV Live on my mobile device?

: Yes, CCTV Live is compatible for mobile devices using 'google chrome' or 'firefox' browser.
It allows you to access remote cameras from mobile devices such as cell phones and tablets. CCTV Live supports devices Android, IOS.

Although it is recommended to use 'google chrome' or 'firefox' browser in mobile devices.
Google Chrome mobile is available free at Google Play Store

2. How can I verify if CCTV Live will work on my device?

  • To verify CCTV Live support, please open this link in Google Chrome. If your device supports CCTV Live you will see video from your webcam/rear cam.

3. What should I use: Firefox or Google Chrome?

  • As you like, CCTV Live works on both browsers well.

4. I have limited Internet bandwidth. Will CCTV Live impact other Internet users in my home?

: No, CCTV Live will not impact Internet users in your home because it needs only upload bandwidth.

5. What is the required upload bandwidth?

: The upload bandwidth depends on video resolution as well as video quality based on webcam you used.

6. I have old computer. Will it work with CCTV Live?

: Yes, CCTV Live has very small hardware requirements.

7. How long the transmission is maintained after I start webcam?

: The standard life time for transmission depends on system ideal time. The transmission may be closed earlier if there is power outage or failure of your Internet access.

8. I have multiple webcams. How can I select one for CCTV Live (Google Chrome)?

: In Google Chrome open:
  1. Settings
  2. Show advanced settings...
  3. Privacy
  4. Content settings
  5. Scroll to Media section
  6. In drop down menu "Microphone" and "Camera" you can select webcam you want to use.
